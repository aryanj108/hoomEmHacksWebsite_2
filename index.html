What is Hook 'Em Hacks?

Hook 'Em Hacks is a 24-hour, beginner-friendly AI hackathon where students come together for a weekend of building, learning, and creativity through technology. Participants from all majors and experience levels can form teams (or work solo), attend hands-on workshops, get guidance from mentors, and turn ideas into real AI projects across multiple impact-focused tracks.
