About

Hook 'Em Hacks is UT Austin's premier beginner-first AI hackathon, taking place this April. Over 24 hours, 250+ students will dive into building AI-powered solutions that matter. Choose from five specialized tracks—AI for Social Good, Multimodal Search & Generation, Security in an AI-First World, Intelligent Financial & Market Systems, and Patient-Centered Tech—and bring your ideas to life.

Never written a line of code? No problem. Whether you're flying solo or teaming up with friends (up to four people), you'll have access to hands-on workshops and 25+ expert mentors ready to help you succeed. We've got you covered with prizes, raffles, swag, and free food all weekend long.

This event welcomes everyone—all majors, all backgrounds, all skill levels. 70% of our hackers are freshmen and sophomores, and half are coding for the first time. Join judges and industry sponsors as you tackle real problems in healthcare, finance, security, and social good. You've got 24 hours to make an impact.

FAQs

Who can attend?

▼

Hook 'em Hacks is open to all current college students, regardless of major or experience level. High school students are welcome with parental consent.

How much does it cost?

▼

Hook 'em Hacks is completely FREE! We'll provide meals, snacks, swag, and everything you need for an amazing weekend of hacking.

What should I bring?

▼

Bring your laptop, chargers, valid student ID, and enthusiasm! We recommend bringing a sleeping bag if you plan to rest during the event.

Do I need a team?

▼

Teams can have up to 4 people. You can come with a team or form one at the event during our team formation session. Solo hackers are also welcome!

What can I build?

▼

Anything! Web apps, mobile apps, hardware projects, games, AI/ML projects—if you can code it, you can build it. We'll have various tracks and prizes.

Have more questions? We're here for you! Send us an email at hello@hookemhacks.com, or reach out to us on Instagram or LinkedIn!
