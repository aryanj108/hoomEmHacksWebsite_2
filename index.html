What is Hook 'Em Hacks?

Hook 'Em Hacks is a 24-hour, beginner-friendly AI hackathon where students come together for a weekend of building, learning, and creativity through technology. Participants from all majors and experience levels can form teams (or work solo), attend hands-on workshops, get guidance from mentors, and turn ideas into real AI projects across multiple impact-focused tracks. Whether it’s your first hackathon or you’ve built before, Hook ’Em Hacks gives you the support, community, and resources to create something meaningful from scratch.

FAQs

Who can attend?

▼

Hook 'em Hacks is open to all current college students, regardless of major or experience level. High school students are welcome with parental consent.

How much does it cost?

▼

Hook 'em Hacks is completely FREE! We'll provide meals, snacks, swag, and everything you need for an amazing weekend of hacking.

What should I bring?

▼

Bring your laptop, chargers, valid student ID, and enthusiasm! We recommend bringing a sleeping bag if you plan to rest during the event.

Do I need a team?

▼

Teams can have up to 4 people. You can come with a team or form one at the event during our team formation session. Solo hackers are also welcome!

What can I build?

▼

Anything! Web apps, mobile apps, hardware projects, games, AI/ML projects—if you can code it, you can build it. We'll have various tracks and prizes.

Have more questions? We're here for you! Send us an email at hello@hookemhacks.com, or reach out to us on Instagram or LinkedIn!
